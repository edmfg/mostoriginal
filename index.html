The Idea

The cover used to belong to magazines. Now it belongs to you.

Every week, fans style their best head-to-toe adidas Originals look. The community votes. And every week the single most-voted fit is crowned the Most Original — it takes over the adidas.com masthead, shoppable and credited.

The name does double duty: it's the brand and the prize. Be the Most Original.

The Magic Pointer

Your cursor just got a stylist.

Magic Pointer is the Googlebook's Gemini-powered cursor — select anything on screen to ask Gemini. Point at it and the cursor blooms into a contextual action chip; pick pieces and they pull onto your canvas; Gemini renders the full fit on you — see your vision, not a mannequin. adidas.com is built pointer-legible, so these default actions map straight onto the Most Original flow. No product change — promotional use of a feature that already ships.
